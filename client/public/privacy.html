Privacy Policy

Guan Dan, Btw is a free multiplayer card game available on the web at guandan.dev and as an iOS app. This policy covers both.

What we collect

Nothing is collected or stored about you. When you play, the display name you type is sent to the game server so other players at your table can see it. It is held in memory only for the duration of your game session and is never written to a database, linked to your identity, or shared with anyone.

Accounts

There are no accounts, no sign ups, and no passwords.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third party SDKs. We do not track you.

Network

The app connects to the game server at guandan.dev over an encrypted connection to play games. Standard, transient connection information (such as your IP address) is used only to deliver the game and is not logged for any other purpose.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Contact

Questions? Reach the developer through guandan.dev.
